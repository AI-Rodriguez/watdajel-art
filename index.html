What we are

Watdajel is a creative and cultural platform born from South American roots and nurtured in Utrecht. We bring together visual art, music, performance, and ideas as one living conversation.

Since our first festival, we've activated monumental spaces across the city, from churches to hidden courtyards, turning them into temporary worlds where emerging and established artists share the same stage.

We create the conditions for great art to happen.

The Space

Located in the center of Utrecht, our space is both a broedplaats and a stage. Surrounded by art and plants, it serves as a workspace for creators, a venue for exhibitions, and a meeting ground for anyone who believes in the power of making things together.
